About

I have finieshed bachelor's studies in biomedical engineering (computer science specialization), and I am currently working towards getting my master's degree in "Artificial intelligence and Machine learning" at Lodz University of Technology.

In my engineering thesis, I designed and trained custom convolutional neural networks to investigate and demonstrate the possibility of inferring hip joint degeneration in patients without analyzing the joint itself — but instead by examining surrounding areas such as the pelvis and femur bones.

I'm primarily interested in deep learning and machine learning, particularly in the area of computer vision, though I'm also open to exploring other fields of artificial intelligence. I enjoy expanding my knowledge and working on personal projects to gain a deeper understanding of the concepts I've learned. You can find a selection of my completed projects in the "Projects" section.
